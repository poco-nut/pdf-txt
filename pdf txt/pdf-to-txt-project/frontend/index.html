How it works

  1. Select a PDF file by dragging & dropping or clicking the "Choose File" button.
  2. Click "Convert to TXT" to start the conversion process.
  3. Wait a few seconds while we extract text from your PDF.
  4. Download the resulting TXT file automatically.
Note: This tool preserves page breaks and formatting as much as possible. Scanned PDFs (image-based) may not produce accurate text.
